Tennis Surface Study
Exploring importance of tennis surfaces
The Problem
Tennis is one of the very few games that has subtypes according to the surface that its played on. This results in different players developing their skills by targeting one of the surfaces: very few players can be their best on all surfaces.
The Solution
As a part of my Information Visualization class, my team decided to highlight the importance of surfaces in the game of tennis. We also explored how this affects players' performance in matches.
